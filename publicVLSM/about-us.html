ABOUT US

Hawassa university

HU is one of the well-established universities in the country known for its quality education, multidisciplinary and problem-solving researches and a wide range of communityservices. Setting teaching- learning, research and community services as its major pillars, HU strives to realize its vision to become the best university at national level, competent in Africa and internationally renowned by 2025.

Mission

The mission of HU is to advance knowledge, enhance technology creation and transfer, promote effective entrepreneurship, and inculcate a responsible and democratic attitude through training, research, and public services; thereby, contributing to the development of the nation.

Vision

Vision Statement Hawassa University aspires to be the best University in the nation, competent in Africa and internationally renowned by 2025.
